---
title: "DLSS Swapper"
description: "Download, manage, and swap DLSS, FSR, and XeSS DLL versions in your PC games — no game update required."
---

DLSS Swapper is a free Windows tool that lets you upgrade or downgrade the upscaling DLL in any supported game. Pick any version of DLSS, FSR 3.1, or XeSS from the built-in library, swap it into your game, and restore the original at any time.

<Warning>
  Be aware of malicious sites claiming to be DLSS Swapper. The only official download is from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/beeradmoore/dlss-swapper/releases) or via `winget`.
</Warning>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install DLSS Swapper">
    Download from GitHub releases or run `winget install --id=beeradmoore.dlss-swapper -e`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your game libraries">
    DLSS Swapper automatically detects Steam, GOG, Epic Games, Ubisoft Connect, Xbox App, Battle.net, and EA App.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and download DLL versions">
    Open the **Library** tab to see all available DLSS, FSR, and XeSS versions. Download whichever you want to try.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Swap a DLL in your game">
    Find your game in the grid, click it, and choose a downloaded version to swap. DLSS Swapper automatically backs up the original DLL first.
  </Step>
</Steps>
